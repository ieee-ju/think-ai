About the event

“Predicting the future isn’t magic, it’s Artificial Intelligence”- Dave Waters.

We are living in the world of humans and machines. It’s natural that these machines or robots have to be programmed to get them to work according to our instructions. But what if the machine started learning on its own from its experience, started to work like us, feel like us, that too without being explicitly programmed – intriguing isn’t it?

Well, we have news for you! “Think AI: The IEEE Machine Learning Summit 2019” hosted by the IEEE Student Branch of Jadavpur University brings to you a two day workshop on Machine Learning and its applications, presenting to you the opportunity of a lifetime to explore your ideas and come up with solutions to real life challenges. The workshop will have eminent figures in the field of Machine Learning from Industry as its guest speakers who will be guiding us in conducting this event.

Speakers

It’s not often that we get to interact with and learn from the most prominent technology gurus in the industry. As its guest speakers, the summit presents to you some of the most eminent names in the enterprise from Intel, Google, ZS and very renowned Data Scientists in the field of ML. The speaker panellists also feature CEOs of leading companies in Data Science & AI. Join us to have a chance to network with the speakers!

Read more

Hands-on Workshops

The two day summit will be offering rigorous hands on workshops under the able guidance of our invited industry experts, with Day 1 offering an interactive workshop on the basic topics of Machine Learning and algorithms. Its primary focus will be providing an overview and developing a basic understanding of principles of ML among the participants. Day 2 will witness an advanced workshop on Machine Learning and applications with Reinforcement Learning as one of the main topics.

Read more

Competition

Day 1 workshop will end with a competition for the participants, with exciting prizes up for grabs for the winners. Whether you’re just learning to code or you’re a seasoned Machine Learning enthusiast, it’s your analytical skills that’ll get you through! Day 2 advanced workshop will end with a final competition, whose winners will be evaluated by our judges, with amazing internship and research opportunities for our top performers. So guys, come forward and give it your best shot!

Read more

Internships

The main highlight of the workshop is the competition that will be conducted, which gives you the perfect chance to apply the knowledge that you have gained and showcase your talent. Winners of the competition, evaluated on the basis of their performance will be awarded prestigious internship and research opportunities in top tech giants, free IEEE membership & certificates recognised by IEEE, and the chance to network with industry experts.
